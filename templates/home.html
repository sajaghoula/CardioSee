CardioSee

CardioSee is a modular, web-based platform for visualizing and analyzing medical data, integrating structured clinical information with computed tomography (CT) images. It enables interactive exploration of population-level datasets, supports statistical analysis, and provides dynamic visualization of images and derived metrics.

Designed for researchers, clinicians, and students, the platform emphasizes data privacy, modularity, and responsive interfaces, while laying the groundwork for secure image processing and segmentation pipelines.
