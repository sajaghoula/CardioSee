CardioSee

An interactive web platform for medical data visualization, statistical analysis, and advanced exploration of medical images with a focus on cardiovascular imaging.

CardioSee is designed to support medical researchers, clinicians, and students in exploring population-level clinical data and computed tomography (CT) images. The platform integrates structured data analysis with multi-planar image visualization and lays the foundation for automated cardiac image segmentation and 3D analysis.
